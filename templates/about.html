About Saaransh Link

The most advanced URL shortening service with comprehensive analytics, inspired by the Nepali word "Saaransh" meaning summary

Our Mission

We believe that every link should tell a story. Our mission is to provide the most comprehensive URL shortening service that not only makes your links shorter but also gives you deep insights into how they perform.

Whether you're a marketer tracking campaign performance, a business owner sharing links with customers, or an individual who wants to understand their audience better, Saaransh Link provides the tools you need.

Key Features

  • Custom short URL aliases
  • Detailed click analytics
  • Geographic tracking
  • QR code generation
  • Link expiration dates
  • Export analytics data
  • REST API access
